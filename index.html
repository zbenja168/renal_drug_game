How to play
  • You'll be shown one drug at a time. For each field (Class → MOA → Uses → AEs → Notes), pick the correct card from 3 choices.
  • Wrong choice flashes red and disables — try one of the other two. Right choice locks green and advances.
  • Completed cards for the current drug stay visible below as a quick recap before you move to the next drug.
  • Use Search / Class to focus on specific drugs. Drugs / round sets how many to study.
  • Skip drug jumps to the next drug without completing.
